Types of Computers

From massive supercomputers that perform billions of calculations per second to the smartphones in our pockets, computers come in all shapes and sizes.

Common types include:

  • Personal Computers (PCs): Desktops and laptops for everyday use.
  • Servers: Powerful machines that "serve" data to other computers on a network.
  • Embedded Systems: Small computers hidden inside other devices, like cars and microwaves.
